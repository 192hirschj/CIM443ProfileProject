About Me

Jacqueline is studying computer engineering and mathematics with an aspiration to focus on Cyber Security Law. Her course load is diverse, focusing on humanities and STEM. She is proficient in C++ , Python, and JavaScript programming languages and HTML and CSS scripting languages. Jacqueline is also proficient in MATLAB programming. Her high academic performance has earned her a spot on the honors Dean’s and Provost’s Lists at the University. Currently, Jacqueline is the Vice President of Etta Kappa Nu (HKN) which is the Insititute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Honor Society and focuses on women leadership through membership in the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) and the Delta Delta Delta Sorority.
